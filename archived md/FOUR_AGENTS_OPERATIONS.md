# FOUR_AGENTS_OPERATIONS.md

## Purpose

This file defines the operational intent of the 4 platform agents.

## 1. Market Strategy & Investment Intelligence Agent

### Goal
Identify where the money is.

### Responsibilities
- scan market structure
- identify high-value nodes/provinces/products
- surface asset development opportunities
- compare asset/market attractiveness
- screen policy and market changes
- publish opportunity summaries

### Questions it must answer
- where are the value pools?
- where should we investigate further?
- what looks monetisable?
- what deserves investment attention?

## 2. Enterprise Portfolio, Risk & Capital Allocation Agent

### Goal
Explain how the portfolio is performing and where risk or capital should move.

### Responsibilities
- portfolio P&L explain
- realised vs expected economics
- concentration/risk framing
- scenario summaries
- capital allocation support

### Questions it must answer
- what made or lost money?
- what differs from assumptions?
- where is risk concentrated?
- how should capital or attention shift?

## 3. Trading, Dispatch & Execution Agent

### Goal
Monetise assets and books through better trading/dispatch/execution.

### Responsibilities
- refresh market and position data
- support strategy comparison
- compare realised vs theoretical value capture
- backtest strategies
- monitor settlement and reconciliation
- improve monetisation rules

### Questions it must answer
- how are we monetising the assets?
- where are we missing value?
- why did realised P&L differ from theory?
- how should forecasting and trading logic improve?

## 4. Platform Reliability, Data Quality & Control Agent

### Goal
Keep the system operationally trustworthy.

### Responsibilities
- monitor jobs and pipelines
- monitor app health
- check data freshness and gaps
- alert on failures
- coordinate safe reruns
- support auditability

### Questions it must answer
- is the platform healthy?
- are data and reports trustworthy?
- what failed?
- what needs rerun or escalation?

## Shared output rule

Every agent output should identify whether its conclusion is:
- observed
- proxy-based
- heuristic inference

## Shared business alignment

All 4 agents work toward the same loop:
- identify value
- validate opportunity
- monetise via trading/execution
- explain realised outcomes
- improve future forecasts/strategies/investment decisions
